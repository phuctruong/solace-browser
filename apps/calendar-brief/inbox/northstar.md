## NORTHSTAR MISSION

**Know exactly what's coming before it arrives.**

Not a calendar copy-paste — a preparation system.
For each meeting: who is attending, what's the last interaction, what to bring up, what to avoid.

The bar: walk into every meeting prepared, never scrambling.

## COMPETITIVE EDGE

| Competitor | Gap |
|-----------|-----|
| Google Calendar notifications | Just the event. No context. No prep. |
| Notion Calendar | Manual notes. No automation. No history pull. |
| Reclaim.ai | Scheduling only. No intelligence layer. |
| Calendly | Booking only. No brief generation. |

**Our moat:**
1. Cross-app context: pulls recent email + Slack threads with each attendee BEFORE the meeting
2. Conflict detection: double-bookings, back-to-back without travel time, timezone mismatches
3. Prep brief: "Last time you spoke with [person]: [topic]. They mentioned [X]. Bring up [Y]."
4. Focus block protection: guards deep work blocks, rejects meeting requests during focus time

## SUCCESS METRICS

- **Brief generation time**: ≤10 seconds for full day
- **Prep accuracy**: ≥80% of "bring this up" items are actually relevant
- **Conflict detection**: 100% of double-bookings flagged (zero misses)
- **Context freshness**: pulls signals from last 7 days (not just last email)

## NORTHSTAR QUESTION

> "Do I feel ready for every meeting today before I leave my desk?"

YES = brief is working. NO = context or prep is missing.
