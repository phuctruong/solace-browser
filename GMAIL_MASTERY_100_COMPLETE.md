### Critical Patterns: 10+

1. **Human-Like Typing** - 80-200ms delays beat bot detection
2. **Autocomplete Interception** - Must press Enter to close dropdown
3. **Explicit Field Clicks** - Tab navigation fails, need click()
4. **Keyboard Shortcuts** - Ctrl+Enter more reliable than click
5. **Session Persistence** - Skip OAuth, reuse 47 cookies
6. **Cookie Lifespan** - 7-30 day validity, auto-refresh every 7d
7. **Rate Limiting** - 1-2 emails/min prevents spam flags
8. **Field Timing** - Wait 1-3s between major transitions
9. **Error Recovery** - Detect expired session, re-auth automatically
10. **Bot Detection Evasion** - Use random delays, avoid instant fills
