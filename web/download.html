Build characteristics

  • Local-first profile
    Cookies and session state stay on your machine by default.
  • OAuth3 vault hooks
    Tokens stay scoped and auditable before recipes use them.
  • Machine + tunnel surfaces
    Disabled until you grant narrower permissions.
