How the Tunnel Works
Step 1
Grant Scope
OAuth3 machine.tunnel scope is required. Grant once from your token settings.
Step 2
Open Tunnel
Solace Browser opens a secure WebSocket tunnel to solaceagi.com. No port forwarding needed.
Step 3
Get Public URL
You receive a unique public URL (e.g. tunnel.solaceagi.com/abc123). Share it with your cloud twin or team.
Step 4
Disconnect Anytime
Click Disconnect or revoke the scope. The tunnel closes instantly. No residual access.
