How every app works

Universal contract: inbox customizes, outbox shows AI work, you approve.

1. Inbox You customize: prompts, templates, assets, policies. Drop files in inbox/ to teach the AI.
2. Preview LLM called ONCE. Preview shown in Yinyang rail. You approve, edit, or decline.
3. Execute Approved output sealed (chmod 444). CPU replays deterministically. Evidence hash-chained.
